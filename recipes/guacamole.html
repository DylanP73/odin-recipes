Guacamole Recipe

Make this last, when you are close to eating.

Avocados don't last too long when they are exposed. If you were to make this the day before, it would be brown and probably not a good idea any more.

For freshness (And entertainment I guess), Guacamole is usually made at the table for you in many Mexican restaurants. Some people seem to think it's unnecessary but I personally love it (And you get to see how fresh the avocados are too).

Ingredients

This recipe makes a good heaped bowl.

  • 6 Avocados (Soft not too mushy)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (Chopped really fine)
  • Half a Teaspoon Salt
  • Half a Teaspoon Pepper
  • Half a Lemon (Juiced)
  • Half a Lemon (Juiced)
  • A couple of dashes of Hot Sauce (Optional)
  • Just under half a cup of the Pico De Gallo

Method

Really simple. just chop up the avocado into small pieces, add all the ingredients and the Pico De Gallo. Mush everything together and add to a serving bowl.
